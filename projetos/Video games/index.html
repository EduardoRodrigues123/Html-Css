The history of the video games

The history of video games, dates back to the early 1950s, when academic computer scientists began designing simple games and simulations as part of their reserach. Over the next two decades,video games evolved from simple text-based computer games to games featuring complex graphics and virtual worlds populated by many players simultaneously.Today, video games are a major form of entertainment, with millions of people playing them around the world
